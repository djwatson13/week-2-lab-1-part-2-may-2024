Fish

According to biologist Stephen Jay Gould, the term "fish" is a misnomer and incorrect, as almost none of the sealife we associate as fish are closely related to each other on an evolutionary scale. However, fish are still very beautiful and fascinating creatures!

Many fish are quite colorful and elegant.
